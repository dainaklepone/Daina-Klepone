KRITUSIŲ MAZGAS

2022 Winner of the Alma Littera fiction competition

In Berlin, amidst the refugee crisis of 2015, the murder investigation of a journalist unveils a web of historical crimes, far-right extremism, and corporate corruption, weaving together a decades-long tale of deadly greed. A crime drama set in a specific historical context—the European refugee crisis of 2015. The story explores universal themes of striving for a better life: some seek love, some money, and others safety. How much are we willing to sacrifice for a better future, and where are the limits of human greed? Germany’s migration policies during this time fueled the rise of far-right radicalism across Europe, while corrupt politicians opened the gates to illicit Russian money.

Recognition 2022: Winner of the "Alma Littera" fiction competition.

2023: Selected by experts for the recommended reading list in Lithuania's "National Book of the Year" competition.

2024: Recognized as one of the most cinematic works of the year in Lithuania.

THE RINGS OF MAYA

COMING IN 2026. When women stop nourishing the earth with their blood, the earth begins to demand the blood of men. One woman, four identities, three generations of secrets. “The Rings of Maja” is a psychological crime novel in which a family tragedy spanning several generations unfolds at the pace of a thriller. This book is a story of guilt, inherited pain, mental illness, and the intertwining of historical memory with personal experience—woven together with the crimes of the modern world.
